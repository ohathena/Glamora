About Glamora

Breaking down unrealistic standards of perfection by making makeup that celebrates uniqueness and promotes self-acceptance.

Our Mission

Glamora was born from a desire to challenge conventional beauty standards. We believe that makeup should be about enhancing your natural beauty, not hiding it.

Founded by Selena Gomez in 2020, our brand is built on the principles of self-acceptance, mental health advocacy, and creating products that work for everyone.

We're not just a beauty brand—we're a movement that encourages people to embrace what makes them glamorous.

A Message from Selena

"I wanted to start a conversation about how beauty can be a source of confidence, not anxiety. Glamora isn't about being someone else, but being who you are."

"Mental health is something I'm passionate about, and I wanted to create a brand that could make a real difference in people's lives."

— Selena Gomez, Founder
